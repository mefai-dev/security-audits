## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Public on-chain data reveals the following verifiable facts regarding the RaveDAO (`$RAVE`) ERC-20 token contract on Ethereum mainnet:

- **89.74%** of total RAVE supply is held in **three Gnosis Safe multisig wallets** (source: Arkham Intelligence public entity data).
- **24.8%** of supply is publicly reported as circulating, producing a **low-float** market structure.
- A **769,699,999 RAVE transfer to a vesting contract** was offset by an equivalent return to treasury control on the same day, producing no net change in effective control.
- **Multiple multi-hop transfer chains** link treasury Safes to labelled centralized-exchange deposit addresses. One deposit of **3,000,000 RAVE was signed by a Treasury Safe signer and delivered to a Gate.io deposit address on 12 April 2026**, during the active price appreciation window.
- The token appreciated from approximately **$0.25 (6 April 2026) to an all-time high of $27.89В·В·В·$28.31 (18 April 2026)** В·В·В· a multiple of approximately **112x** in 12 days.
- Public futures data reflects approximately **$120MВ·В·В·$140M in cumulative liquidations** over the 7-day appreciation window, of which approximately **74% were short positions** (source: Coinglass).

Every primary-evidence transaction hash cited in this report was independently resolved against **at least three of four independent block explorers** (Etherscan, OKLink, Blockscan, 3xpl). The single highest-tier item В·В·В· the 3,000,000 RAVE Gate.io deposit of 12 April 2026 signed by Treasury Safe Signer #4 В·В·В· was confirmed on **all four** explorers with all fields (signer, amount, recipient label, timestamp, block) matching. ## 14. OBSERVED PATTERNS (FACTUAL, NEUTRAL FRAMING)

1. **Supply concentration.** Three Gnosis Safe addresses hold ~89.74% of issued supply (Arkham, public data).
2. **Low circulating float.** Approximately 24.8% circulating supply, producing a market structure in which modest order flow can produce large price movements.
3. **Multi-hop routing.** Multiple verified transaction chains show token transfers from treasury Safes to centralized exchanges via 3В·В·В·4 intermediate addresses rather than direct deposits.
4. **Timing correlation.** A 3,000,000 RAVE Gate.io deposit (Section 8) was authored by a Treasury Safe signer on the same day as Treasury Safe #1 multisig nonces 19 and 20 (4.44M RAVE each to Safe #3), during the active price-appreciation window.
5. **Short-squeeze mechanics.** Public Coinglass data shows that the majority (~74%) of liquidations were short positions and that funding rates reached -1,200% to -2,400% annualized В·В·В· mechanics consistent with a short-squeeze dynamic on a low-float token.
6. **Direct outreach without response.** On-chain investigator **ZachXBT** publicly stated (X, April 2026) that he contacted the RaveDAO co-founder eight hours before publishing his concerns; the message was read but not replied to (ZachXBT public post).
